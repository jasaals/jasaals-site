# JASAALS Was Born

Before we were engaged, before we knew exactly what our future would look like, there was a moment of quiet certainty.

I bought **jasaals.com**—a domain that didn't exist yet but felt like it already belonged to us. Jeffrey Alexander Spears + Anna Leah Spears. JASAALS. A name for whatever we'd build together.

## Why a domain?

It might seem like a strange gesture, but for me it was a stake in the ground. A digital plot of land for our future. Somewhere we could eventually share our story, our photos, our dreams.

> "Names have power. They shape identity. They become real."

At the time, I didn't know what JASAALS would become. A wedding site? A family blog? A private archive of our life? Maybe all of those things, eventually.
